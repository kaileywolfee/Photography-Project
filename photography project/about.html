Here at Doggo Photography, we are
dedicated to taking quality pictures of dogs, rare dogs,
and sometimes even non-dogs. Our star
doggo is Luna, who resides in James Island.
Luna's favorite activities include eating
garbage, taking naps, going on walks, and
chasing birds on Folly Beach. Although we
mainly take pictures of Luna, we pride our
organization on taking photos of any and all
good puppers. We are a non-profit
organization, and all of our procedes go to
taking more quality pupper pics. If you would
like to be featured on our website, feel free
to contact us and we will get back to you as
soon as possible.
